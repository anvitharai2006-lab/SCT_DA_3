## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition patterns to identify factors contributing to workforce turnover. The dashboard provides insights into attrition across departments, job roles, age groups, income levels, gender, and job satisfaction.

## Tools Used

- Tableau
- Excel

## Key Metrics

- Total Employees
- Employees Left
- Average Age

## Dashboard Insights

- Research & Development experienced the highest attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives showed the highest turnover.
- Employees aged 25–35 had the highest attrition levels.
- Lower income groups showed higher employee turnover.
- Job satisfaction demonstrated a relationship with attrition.

## Outcome

The dashboard helps HR teams identify high-risk employee groups and supports data-driven retention strategies and provides answer to questions :
"why are employees leaving?" 
"which factors correlate most happiness?" .
